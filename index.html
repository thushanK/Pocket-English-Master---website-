Personalized education Everybody learns in a unique way. In order to design the most efficient educational system and individually cater it to each student, we can now, for the first time in history, examine how millions of individuals simultaneously learn.
Our ultimate objective is to use technology to make private tutoring accessible to everyone.

  • Making learning fun It's hard to stay motivated when learning online, so we made Pocket English Master so fun that people would prefer picking up new skills over playing a game.
  • Universally accessible
  • Over 1.2 billion individuals are learning a language, and most of them do so to open up more options. Unfortunately, most people cannot afford and cannot learn a language.
  • To give everyone an opportunity, we built Pocket English Master. Free language instruction-there are no extra costs or premium features.

Our teaching approach
With Pocket English Master, we think anybody can learn a language. Our free, bite-sized courses are designed to seem more like games than textbooks since having fun while learning makes learning easier.

Pocket English Master, however, is more than a game. It is founded on a technique that has been shown to promote long-term retention and on a curriculum that adheres to a global standard. Let's investigate how Pocket English Master facilitates language acquisition.

A balanced approach
In a similar fashion to how you acquired your native tongue as a youngster, Pocket English Master enables students to identify patterns on their own without having to concentrate on grammar rules. This method, known as "implicit learning," is excellent for gaining a firm understanding of a language's fundamental concepts and laws.

But for some topics, specific education is helpful. Therefore, Pocket English Master provides both. Learners also have access to Tips, which include explanations of grammar, pronunciation, and useful expressions.

Primary objective Primary objective of this research is to develop a mobile application using machine learning, reinforcement learning and augmented reality to enhance the quality of English learning.
