Our teaching approach
With Pocket English Master, we think anybody can learn a language. Our free, bite-sized courses are designed to seem more like games than textbooks since having fun while learning makes learning easier.

Pocket English Master, however, is more than a game. It is founded on a technique that has been shown to promote long-term retention and on a curriculum that adheres to a global standard. Let's investigate how Pocket English Master facilitates language acquisition.

A balanced approach
In a similar fashion to how you acquired your native tongue as a youngster, Pocket English Master enables students to identify patterns on their own without having to concentrate on grammar rules. This method, known as "implicit learning," is excellent for gaining a firm understanding of a language's fundamental concepts and laws.

But for some topics, specific education is helpful. Therefore, Pocket English Master provides both. Learners also have access to Tips, which include explanations of grammar, pronunciation, and useful expressions.
